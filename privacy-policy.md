Privacy Policy

Kivo Notes – Simple & Fast Notes

Last updated: January 2026

HC Creative Studio built Kivo Notes – Simple & Fast Notes as a free application. This app is provided at no cost and is intended for personal note-taking use.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the app.

Information Collection and Use

Kivo Notes – Simple & Fast Notes does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

All notes created within the app are stored locally on your device only. The app does not require an internet connection and does not send any data to external servers.

Data Storage

All notes are stored locally on your device.

No data is uploaded, synced, or backed up to the cloud.

HC Creative Studio does not have access to your notes or any other app data.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored notes, as the data exists only on your device and is not backed up elsewhere.

Data Security

Because all data is stored locally and never transmitted, your notes remain private and accessible only to you through your device.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, no information can be shared or misused.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any updates.
